## Context

Thiga is a consulting firm specialized in Product Management. It staffs consultants in full immersion at scale-ups — not advisory-only work, but real delivery with full product ownership. The detail of each mission (Kolecto, Electra, Ledger) lives in its own case study; this one covers the method I apply across all of them.

## What I bring on a mission

**Immediate ownership** — no warm-up period. Understanding the product context, aligning stakeholders, and shipping first milestones within the first weeks.

**Structured discovery** — user interviews, problem mapping, prioritization. On every mission, rigorous exploration work before specifying anything.

**Frictionless delivery** — I adapt my methods to the organization in place (Shape Up, Agile, OKRs) rather than imposing a framework. What matters: the team moves forward.

## What I learned

Consulting PM work forces you to perform in very different contexts, each time with a short learning curve and high expectations. It's what taught me the most about my ability to generate value quickly — regardless of domain or stack.

The diversity of missions (FinTech, GreenTech, Crypto) also broadened my range: e-invoicing regulation, electric mobility, DeFi — all contexts that force you to go straight to the essential, fast.
